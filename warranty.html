Warranty

Service maintenance and repair of cosmetology equipment

Repair at the service center

UPDATEMED provides warranty and post-warranty repair of professional equipment. Despite the high quality of the devices, any equipment requires diagnostics, preventive maintenance, and timely replacement of components.

If your device has malfunctioned or requires inspection, please contact our specialists!

Why choose us?

Professional approach

A team of qualified engineers with extensive experience in cosmetology equipment servicing.

Original spare parts

We use only certified components with an official warranty.

Service flexibility

Repairs can be carried out at our service center in Moscow or on-site by prior agreement.

Process transparency

Fixed pricing, strict deadlines, and a warranty on all completed work.

What equipment do we service?
  1. Laser systems (diode, Nd:YAG, fractional CO2)
  2. Photoepilation devices
  3. Cryolipolysis and pressotherapy equipment
  4. Devices for cavitation, hydrafacial treatments, SMAS lifting, and more

The year of manufacture and model do not matter — our team works with all types of equipment.

Repair stages
  1. Initial consultation and diagnostics.
  2. Decision on on-site repair or service center repair.
  3. Selection of spare parts; if necessary, ordering from the manufacturer.
  4. Repair work (up to 5 days if components are in stock).
  5. Warranty for the completed repair.

Fast and reliable service

We understand that every day of equipment downtime means lost opportunities for your business. That is why we carry out repairs as quickly as possible — in most cases within 2–3 days.

Contact us for a consultation and a prompt solution to your problem!
