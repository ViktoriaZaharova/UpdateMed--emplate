Training

Comprehensive training for hardware cosmetology specialists from UPDATEMED

Why is training important when purchasing equipment?

Mastering professional equipment requires not only practice but also a competent and structured approach. Our training will help you gain a deeper understanding of how the devices work, learn how to individually select parameters for each client, and follow safety protocols. We also thoroughly cover contraindications for procedures to minimize risks and increase treatment effectiveness.

Even if you already have experience working with similar devices, additional training will help improve your skills and master the nuances of new techniques.

Who conducts the training?

The sessions are conducted by certified cosmetology professionals with many years of practical experience.

Which techniques can you learn?
  • Modern laser hair removal technologies
  • Photoepilation and skin rejuvenation
  • Body contouring аппарат techniques (cryolipolysis, cavitation)
  • Non-surgical tattoo and permanent makeup removal methods
  • Carbon peeling and its specific features
  • Vacuum roller massage and its applications
  • Vascular treatments: effective removal methods
Training formats

On-site training

Conducted at our training center, where you can fully master all features of the equipment in practice.

On-location training

Our expert will visit your salon or clinic and provide training on-site. Travel to any region is possible with prior agreement and payment of transportation expenses.

Online training

Training is conducted via video consultation, where a specialist explains the operating principles of the device in detail. Practical training is not included in this format, but you will receive all the necessary knowledge for independent operation.

Training program

Each format includes both theoretical and practical modules.

Theoretical module

  • Fundamentals of anatomy and physiology
  • Operating principles of various techniques
  • Indications and contraindications
  • Procedure execution algorithms
  • Skin preparation and recovery recommendations
  • Device setup and operation specifics

Practical module

  • Client skin condition diagnostics
  • Procedure preparation algorithm
  • Working with the device interface and settings
  • Procedure performance: demonstration and independent practice
Training duration

Depending on the specialist’s level of experience, the training duration ranges from 1 to 2.5 hours.

Access to a professional community

All course graduates receive access to a closed professional community where they can exchange experience, receive consultations, discuss complex cases, and find new solutions to improve the quality of their work.
